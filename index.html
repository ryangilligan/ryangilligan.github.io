ryan gilligan

is a mix engineer based in los angeles









listen to featured mixes on spotify








Ryan came up in New York at Electric Lady and Quad Studios. He worked alongside Michael Brauer for 7+ years before relocating to LA to work with producer John Hill.



Ryan has been awarded three Grammy Certificates for work done with John Mayer, Kanye West, and Calle 13.



Ryan has worked with artists including Kevin Garrett, Anthony Ramos, Jasmine Cephas-Jones, Cynthia Erivo, rum.gold, Beck Pete, Little Monarch, Chris Ayer, Twin Shadow, Cibo Matto, Coldplay, John Mayer, Kanye West, Grizzly Bear, Charli XCX, Santigold, and Florence and the Machine.
